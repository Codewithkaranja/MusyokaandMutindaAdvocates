Insurance Claims in Kenya: How to Strengthen Your Position

Many claim disputes are caused by missing documents, unclear reporting timelines, or policy exclusions. The key is to document everything early and communicate through clear written records.

Practical steps we recommend

  • Preserve evidence: photos, reports, receipts, medical records, police abstracts where relevant
  • Confirm policy terms: deadlines, exclusions, and notification requirements
  • Keep communication in writing: email/letters to ensure a clean record
  • Get a legal review early: before you sign a settlement or accept a reduced offer

Debt Recovery in Machakos: Professional, Fast, Enforceable

If someone owes you money, you need a plan that protects your cashflow while preserving legal leverage. We start with structured demand notices, then move to negotiation, mediation, or court enforcement when necessary.

Our debt recovery toolkit

  1. Document review: invoices, agreements, delivery notes, payment history
  2. Demand letter: clear facts, legal basis, and deadline
  3. Negotiation: secure a settlement agreement with enforceable terms
  4. Action: filing and enforcement where settlement fails

Frequently Asked Questions

What if an insurer keeps delaying my claim?

Delays often reduce when the matter is properly documented and escalated through a structured demand. We help you present the claim clearly and pursue settlement or dispute resolution where appropriate.

Can I recover a debt without going to court?

Yes. Many debts are resolved through strong demand letters and negotiated settlements. Where a debtor refuses, legal action and enforcement may be necessary.

Should I sign an offered settlement immediately?

Not before review. A quick settlement may have clauses that limit your rights. We advise you before you commit.
